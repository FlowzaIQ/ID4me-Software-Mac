⚠️ Strict CSV Format Required

To ensure the automation works properly, your RPData export must NOT contain any headers.

It must only contain these specific sections in this exact order: Street Address, Sale Date, Owner Name, and Owner Type.

35 GATEHOUSE CIRCUIT | WERRINGTON DOWNS | NSW | 2747 | 09 Jun 2000 | PAUL WALTER PEARSON | BARKER | DOYLE | Owner Occupied

If it is not formatted like this, the scraper will fail.
